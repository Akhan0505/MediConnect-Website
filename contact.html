Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting our "My Bookings" page, selecting your preferred doctor, date, and time slot. Fill in your details and confirm the booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Visit your bookings page to manage your appointments.

Are all doctors verified?

Yes, all doctors on our platform are verified professionals with proper medical credentials and licenses. We ensure quality healthcare services.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes, many of our doctors offer online video consultations. You can select this option when booking your appointment.

How can I become a partner doctor?

If you're a qualified medical professional, please contact us through this form with "Doctor Registration" as the subject. We'll guide you through the registration process.

What if I have technical issues?

For any technical issues, please contact our support team using the form above or call our helpline. We provide 24/7 technical support.
