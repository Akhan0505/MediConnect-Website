Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment by searching for doctors on our homepage, selecting your preferred doctor, and clicking "Book Now". Fill in your details and choose an available time slot to confirm your appointment.

Are all doctors on OlaDoc verified?

Yes, all doctors on our platform are thoroughly verified. We check their medical licenses, qualifications, and professional credentials before allowing them to join our network.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Please contact our support team or use the appointment management feature in your account.

Do you offer video consultations?

Yes, many of our doctors offer video consultations for your convenience. You can choose between in-person visits and video consultations when booking your appointment.

How can I become a doctor on OlaDoc?

Medical professionals can apply to join our platform by contacting us through this form or emailing doctors@oladoc.com. We'll guide you through our verification process and help you set up your profile.
